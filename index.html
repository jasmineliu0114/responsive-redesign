Company History

Icenhower Oil and Gas Inc., founded in 1994 by Tim Icenhower is an oilfield construction and pipeline company headquartered in Bossier City, Louisiana.

We currently operate 60 service crews and 10 pipeline installation crews throughout seven (7) states.

In the oil and gas industry there are numerous organizations who will promise success, but our experience, knowledge, and safety culture permits us to guarantee it!
